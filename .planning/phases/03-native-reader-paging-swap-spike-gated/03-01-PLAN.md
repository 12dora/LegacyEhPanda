<objective>
Establish the Wave-0 regression guard for the DEP-05 swap: a dedicated `ReadingFeatureTests` target
covering `PageHandler.mapToPager`/`mapFromPager` (the pure stack-index ↔ reading-page mapping incl. the
dual-page cover-exception math) and `ReadingReducer.State.containerDataSource` (dual-page / exceptCover
stack collapsing). No such target exists today; existing reading tests are reducer-level in
`DownloadsFeatureTests`. These pure suites are the fast, headless proof that the mapping the reader
consumes is unchanged when its consumer is re-seamed from the SwiftUIPager `Page` to a plain index in
later waves (D-07).

Purpose: `PageHandler` survives the swap byte-for-byte — only its caller changes. Locking its behavior
now means the reader/carousel re-seam cannot silently drift the index math (the central off-by-one
hazard). Per RESEARCH § Validation Architecture and 03-VALIDATION Wave 0 Requirements.

Output: a new test target + two Swift Testing suites, green and sub-second. No production code changes.

Implements the Wave 0 test-scaffolding step (RESEARCH primary recommendation) and CONTEXT § Specifics
(add a dedicated PageHandler suite — none exists today).
</objective>
