<background>
Plan 01-07 delivered DEP-07 by updating Colorful to the official `Lakr233/Colorful.git`
exact `1.1.1`, but Colorful 1.1.x marks the whole `ColorfulView` struct deprecated on
non-watchOS ("This library hurts CPU alot, use Metal program from
https://github.com/Lakr233/ColorfulX instead") with no in-package replacement. 01-07 was
scoped to forbid an alternative gradient path, so the residual warning was documented — not
suppressed — as a user-decision blocker in `01-COLORFUL-UAT.md` with three options: (a)
migrate to ColorfulX, (b) an app-owned SwiftUI gradient view, or (c) accept the warning.

The user selected option (a). ColorfulX 6.1.0 exposes a SwiftUI `ColorfulView` whose
motion is driven by a `speed` binding (0 freezes) rather than an `animated: Bool` flag, and
a non-binding `init(color: [Color], speed:…)` convenience that consumes the existing
extracted `[Color]` directly. The migration stays inline in `GalleryCardCell` and does not
touch `HomeReducer.rawCardColors`/`cardColors` or `LibraryClient.analyzeImageColors`.
</background>
