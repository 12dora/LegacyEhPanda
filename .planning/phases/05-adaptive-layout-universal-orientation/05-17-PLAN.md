<objective>
Close two G-05-4 defects about iPad windowing:

- Defect 8: neither the app/root nor Home sets an explicit content background, while Home's Other
  cards use `Color(.systemGray6)`. When the platform window surface is a grouped/sheet gray, the
  backdrop matches the cards and their boundaries vanish. Establish a visually distinct semantic
  root surface at appearance parity in normal windows.
- Defect 9 (user-locked): `App/Info.plist` advertises `UIApplicationSupportsMultipleScenes=true`,
  but every `WindowGroup` renders `RootView(appDelegate:)` from the single `AppDelegate.store`, so
  all windows share navigation/tab/lock/feature state. Disable multiple-scene support so the app no
  longer advertises unsupported multi-window.

Purpose: Home cards stay visible in iPad windows, and the app stops advertising a multi-window
capability it does not support.
Output: distinct Home root surface (normal-window parity); multi-scene support disabled.
</objective>
